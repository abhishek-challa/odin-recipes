Biryani

Biryani is one of the most famous rice dishes of india. It can be made various different ways, now we will look what goes into making a biryani

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Garam masala
  • Stock
  • Lemon juice
  • Yoghurt
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Red chilli powder
  1. First prepare the chicken marinade using spices
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Saute the chicken until dark brown
  4. place the sauted chicken botton of the pot
  5. place your rice on top of the chicken
  6. cook for an hour

Now your chicken biryani is ready!and done
